We at Self Made Solutions believe that

An effective website needs to be more than just a pretty face. As well as looking good, it should be simple for you and your colleagues to use; it should be easy for your web users to find what you want them to find.

I strive to design good looking, 'sexy' websites which are visually stunning and friendly to both humans and search engines. Whether it is a static HTML based website or a feature rich Flash site, when it comes to world-class web designs, you can trust me to deliver you simply the best.

In my perspective; a website should have a unique personality which apparently stands out. Websites ought to be extremely effective and are well thought out.

Of course, good design is subjective, however as someone who spends much of my time seeing what resonates, I think I have developed some aesthetic sense for what works. Consequently, I have developed four guiding principles that I believe form the core of any successful Web site—usability, aesthetics, visibility, and flexibility. These aren't just buzzwords; combined, these are a comprehensive set of blueprints needed to craft a Web presence that will positively impact business. So no need to mention I do adhere with this.
